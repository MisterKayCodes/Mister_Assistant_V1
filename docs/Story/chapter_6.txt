# Chapter 6: The Keeper of Hours

Inside the Archivist's (Repository) great basement, there is a specialized scribe called **The Keeper of Hours**. He handles the **Activities**.

When The Riddler (Parser) says "Start Activity," The Keeper of Hours is summoned. He opens the vault and writes a **New Line of Existence**:

```python
    def start_activity(self, user_id, name):
        cursor = self.conn.cursor()
        start_time = datetime.now()
        cursor.execute(
            "INSERT INTO activities (user_id, name, start_time) VALUES (?, ?, ?)",
            (user_id, name, start_time)
        )
        activity_id = cursor.lastrowid
        self.conn.commit()
        ...
        return activity_id
```

But his true masterwork is **The Paradox of Switching**. When you switch activities, the Keeper must freeze time for the old one and calculate its life span (the duration) before breathing life into the new one:

```python
    def stop_activity(self, activity_id):
        cursor = self.conn.cursor()
        end_time = datetime.now()
        ... # Calculate total seconds
        duration = int((end_time - start_time).total_seconds())
        cursor.execute(
            "UPDATE activities SET end_time = ?, duration = ? WHERE id = ?",
            (activity_id, duration)
        )
        self.conn.commit()
```

He is the reason you know how much of your life was spent "Coding" and how much was spent "Resting." He is the accountant of your very existence.
