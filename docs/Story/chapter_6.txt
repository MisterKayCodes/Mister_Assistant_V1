# Chapter 6: The Marathon Runner (Activity)

**Mr. Handler** is the first of the four Guild Leaders. He handles **Activity**. 

He is always looking at his watch. When you say "Starting," he runs to the Archivist and says "Open a new page!" He records the exact millisecond you began.

But his true drama is **The Switch**. 
When you say "Now lunch," he has to do three things at once:
1. Stop the old activity.
2. Calculate how long you were doing it.
3. Start the NEW activity.

He is the most active member of the bot, constantly running back and forth to ensure every second of your life is accounted for.
