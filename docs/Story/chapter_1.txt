# Chapter 1: The Heartbeat of the Machine

In the dark void of the folder, **Main.py** woke up. He was the center of everything, the master coordinator.

First, Main needed his "Oxygen"—the **TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN**. He reached into the `.env` scroll using this ancient ritual:

```python
load_dotenv()
TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN = os.getenv("TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN")
```

If the oxygen was missing, Main would die instantly. But once he breathed, he summoned his two generals: **Bot** (the Messenger) and **Dispatcher** (the Traffic Warden).

```python
    bot = Bot(token=TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN)
    dp = Dispatcher()
```

Main didn't want to handle every citizen's request himself. He called for **The Router**, a specialist from the `bot.handlers` district. He told the Warden to listen to the Router:

```python
    dp.include_router(router)
```

Finally, with a loud cry of "🤖 Bot Starting...", Main entered the **Eternal Vigil** (The Polling Loop). He would now wait forever for a message to arrive from the Great Telegram Cloud:

```python
    try:
        await dp.start_polling(bot)
    except Exception as e:
        print(f"[!] Error: {e}")
```

Main was the heart. He kept the blood flowing, but he left the thinking to others.
