# Chapter 7: The Witness of Names (People)

The second Guild Leader is **The Witness**. He doesn't care about time or money; he only cares about **Connections**.

When you say "My sister is Chichi," The Witness steps forward. He takes out a special scroll called **The People Table**. 

"Aha," he says. "A new soul for my directory." 

He records the name and the relationship. He is the Bot's heart. He ensures that the Bot doesn't just treat you as a user, but as a person with a life and family. Without The Witness, the Bot would be a cold Calculator. With him, the Bot is a Companion.
