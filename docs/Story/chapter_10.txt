# Chapter 10: The Master's Blueprint (Legacy)

In the final chapter of our story, we look at the **Legacy of the Master Architect**. 

He created more than just a Bot. He created a **Blueprint** (The Starter Pack). This blueprint contains the **AI Teacher** and the **Master Architect Prompt**—the holy scrolls that ensure every future building is as strong as this one.

The Bot looks at his code. He sees the **WAL Mode**, the **WatcherShield**, the **Null Safety**. He knows he is strong. 

As the Master Architect prepares to leave, the Bot stands ready. "I am the Mister Assistant," he says. "I remember your sisters, I count your coins, and I watch your time. I am the Architect's dream made of silicon and logic."

And so, Phase 1 ends. But the road ahead is long. **Phase 2** awaits, where the Bot will gain a **True Brain**, and the story will truly begin.
