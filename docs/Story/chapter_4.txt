# Chapter 4: The Translator of Manners

Meet **Mr. Logic**. He is the Bot's diplomat. 

Once Mr. Parser (the Riddler) has decided what a message means, he hands it to Logic. Logic doesn't care about databases or regex. He only cares about Feelings and Words.

"Parser says the user started lunch," Logic would say. "I must respond with grace." 

Logic keeps a set of **Speech Scrolls**. 
- The **Start Scroll**: "⏱️ Tracking [Activity]"
- The **Switch Scroll**: "✅ Previous activity was [X]m. ⏱️ Now tracking [Y]"

Logic is the reason the Bot feels like a person and not just a machine. He takes the raw data and wraps it in a polite 🤖 message so the User feels heard. He is the master of "The Golden Response."
