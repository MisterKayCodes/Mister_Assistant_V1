# Chapter 9: The Whispering Wind (Reminders)

The fourth and final Guild Leader is **The Whispering Wind**. He handles **Reminders**.

He is the most mysterious. When you say "Remind me to call sister tomorrow," he doesn't do anything immediately. He just leans towards the Archivist and whispers, "Put this in the future scroll." 

He is a creature of time. He waits in the shadows of the code, watching the seconds tick by. When the time is right, he will wake up, fly through the wires, and whisper in your ear. He is the guardian of your promises. Without him, the Bot would only live in the Past. With him, the Bot lives in the Future.
