# Chapter 9: The Weaving of Souls (The Router)

Back in the upper city of `bot/handlers.py`, **The Great Router** is weaving the many threads of the specialists together. He is the **Middleman of Fate**.

When a message arrives, the Router calls the **Main Handler**. This handler is the "Brain of the Day." It starts with the **Scrubbing Ritual** (Parsing) and then checks the **Scroll of Intent**.

It performs the **Switching Ritual** for Activities:

```python
    if intent == "start_activity":
        repo.start_activity(user_id, parsed.get("name"))
        response = logic.format_start_message(parsed.get("name"))
    
    elif intent == "switch_activity":
        active_id = repo.get_active_activity(user_id)
        duration = None
        if active_id:
            duration = repo.stop_activity(active_id)
        repo.start_activity(user_id, parsed.get("name"))
        response = logic.format_switch_message(duration, parsed.get("name"))
```

The Router is the one who decides WHICH specialist to call. He is the director of the play. If he sees "Spending," he calls the Banker. If he sees "Unknown," he calls the Strategist for an apology. He is the one who turns individual functions into a living, breathing **Bot**.
