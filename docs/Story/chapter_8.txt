# Chapter 8: The Oracle of Tomorrow

The final specialist in the basement is **The Oracle (Repository Reminders)**. He doesn't look at the past; he looks at **The Whispering Future**.

When a user says "Remind me," The Oracle takes a **Parchment of the Future** and writes down the whisper and the moment it should be spoken:

```python
    def set_reminder(self, user_id, text, reminder_date, recurring=False):
        cursor = self.conn.cursor()
        cursor.execute(
            "INSERT INTO reminders (user_id, text, reminder_date, recurring) VALUES (?, ?, ? , ?)",
            (user_id, text, reminder_date, recurring)
        )
        self.conn.commit()
```

The Oracle is a silent waiter. He puts the "whisper" into the vault and waits for the clock to strike. He is the bridge between the Bot's present action and your future destiny. Without him, the Bot would forget everything once the sun sets. With him, the Bot is an Eternal Watcher.
