# Chapter 8: The Banker of PalmPay (Spending)

**The Banker** is the third Guild Leader. He is strict and precise. He handles the **Spending Table**.

He has a special agreement with **Mr. Parser**. Every time a "spent" or "paid" message arrives, The Banker is summoned. 

"₦1000 for transport," he mutters. He records the amount and the category. But he is also a creature of habit. "I shall assume this was done via **PalmPay**," he declares, unless told otherwise.

The Banker ensures that at the end of the month, you know where every coin went. He is the silent accountant living inside the wires.
